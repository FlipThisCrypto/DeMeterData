Plant a Tree

Provision a Tree end-to-end: identify it over USB, register it with the oracle, push WiFi creds, point it at the oracle, and trigger the first sample.

1. Connect over USB

Pick the COM port your Tree is plugged into. If it isn't listed, click Refresh.

2. Verify your Orchard Pass

An Orchard Pass NFT is the on-chain credential that proves you operate a Tree on this network. Paste the Chia wallet address that holds your Pass; the oracle verifies on chain (via the public MintGarden indexer) and binds the Pass to your Tree.

3. Configure the Tree

The Tree will store WiFi credentials in NVS and start POSTing to the oracle URL you set here.
